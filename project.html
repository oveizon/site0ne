The business logic and templating were worked on by me. I also assited in the responsive design and user authentication.

Cubeseed Cubeseed is a virtual marketplace that aims to facilitate agro-trade, boost agricultural production, and empower farmers. The platform will be designed to connect farmers with buyers, investors, manufacturers of agro-inputs, service providers, and processors. These are the 6 user types the app will cater to. The user type I am working on with a fellow frontend developer is the Service Providers user type.
I created the Invoice and Job Certificate pages.
Tools being used are Next.js, Sass, Context Api, and Html.
